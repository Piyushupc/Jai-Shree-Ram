Teachings from Ramayana

  • Dharma (Righteousness): Always follow the path of truth and justice.
  • Respect for Parents: Lord Ram obeyed his father’s command and accepted exile.
  • Devotion & Loyalty: Hanuman and Bharat showcased true devotion.
  • Friendship & Trust: Lord Ram honored his friendship with Sugreeva.
  • Compassion & Forgiveness: Lord Ram forgave enemies like Vibhishana.
